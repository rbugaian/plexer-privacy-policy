# Privacy Policy for Plexer: Display Layouts

Effective Date: 16.06.2026

Plexer: Display Layouts (“Plexer,” “the app,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is designed to help users save, manage, preview, and apply display layout configurations on macOS.

Your privacy is important to us. Plexer is built to work locally on your Mac and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

## 1. Information We Collect

Plexer does not collect personal information.

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

* Your name
* Email address
* Location
* Contact information
* Device identifiers for tracking
* Usage analytics
* Advertising identifiers
* Files or documents
* Screen contents
* Any personal data

## 2. Display Configuration Data

Plexer allows you to save named display layout presets, including monitor arrangements and main display settings.

This configuration data is stored locally on your Mac and is used only to provide the app’s core functionality, such as saving, previewing, editing, applying, and deleting display layout presets.

Your saved layouts are not uploaded to any server and are not accessible to us.

## 3. Local Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device.

Plexer does not use cloud storage, remote databases, third-party analytics services, or external tracking tools.

You can manage or delete saved configurations inside the app.

## 4. Data Sharing

Plexer does not share your data with third parties.

Because the app does not collect or transmit your data, there is no personal data to sell, rent, disclose, or otherwise provide to advertisers, analytics providers, or external services.

## 5. Network Access

Plexer does not require network access to save, manage, preview, or apply display layouts.

The app’s core features operate locally on your Mac.

## 6. Analytics and Tracking

Plexer does not use analytics, tracking technologies, cookies, advertising SDKs, or similar tools.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Plexer does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Since the app does not collect personal data, no personal information from children is collected or stored.

## 8. Data Security

Because Plexer stores configuration data locally on your Mac, the security of that data depends on your device security settings, such as your macOS account password, disk encryption, and system permissions.

We recommend keeping your Mac and macOS installation secure and up to date.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective Date” at the top of this policy.
